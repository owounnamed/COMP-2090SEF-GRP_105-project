### **Team Member Responsibilities template**

#### **Task 1: Research & Planning**

* **Team Member 1**: Responsible for conducting background research on the project's topic, identifying key challenges, and drafting a project plan.

#### **Task 2: Development**

* **Team Member 2**: In charge of coding the main functionality of the application, including the development of the user interface and database integration.
* **Team Member 3**: Responsible for implementing the algorithm and handling backend logic, such as sorting and filtering features.

#### **Task 3: Testing & Quality Assurance**

* **Team Member 4**: Responsible for writing test cases, running tests, and identifying bugs in the application to ensure it functions properly before the final submission.

#### **Task 4: Documentation**

* **Team Member 5**: Responsible for preparing and finalizing the project report, including writing the introduction, methodology, and conclusion sections. Also in charge of the **README.md** file and the user guide documentation.

#### **Task 5: Presentation & Video**

* **Team Member 6**: Responsible for creating the project’s introduction video, and coordinating the preparation of the final presentation slides.
